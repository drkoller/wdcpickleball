Thank you for an amazing 2023!

Thank you to all of the DCPL league participants who made 2023 such a fun year of pickleball! From our humble launch in April, DCPL grew throughout the year to eventually comprise 5 "seasons" and 27 different league sessions. This could only have happened with the enthusiastic support of the hundreds of players who made it all possible. Check out our Instagram for a visual record of the year on the courts, or see the table below that remembers our various league champions of 2023:

In our social leagues in 2023, Aris was a 3-time ladder champion, and Marc B was a 3-time playoff champion. In the competitive league category, Evan and George both won 3 ladder league championships apiece. George achieved the highest ladder rating in DCPL history, compiling an incredible 109-42 record in shootout matches against the toughest opponents that we could find. Congratulations to all of the year's winners!

Our "most active" player of the year honor in the social leagues goes to David R, who played 259 official recorded games in 2023. In the competitive leagues, the award goes to David E, who completed 273 games during 4 seasons of league play (Ben S had 272). And a special shout-out to those players who were with us from our humble beginnings and joined us playing in all 5 seasons this year: Angela, Belkie, Ben S, David R, Erika, Jared, Justin K, Karen G, Meaghan, Rhonda, Sam R, Siobhan, Susan and Suzanne. Thank you all!
