DCPL Juneteenth Partner Tournament

Join us on Friday, June 19 at the Anacostia Park Outdoor Courts for the DC Pickleball League’s (DCPL) Juneteenth Partner Tournament. This is a team-entry doubles event — players must register with a partner. Mixed or same-gender teams are welcome. All players are guaranteed a minimum of 4 games in our round-robin pool plus playoff format.

Format:

Teams in divisions with more than 9 teams will be seeded and separated into multiple pools for round-robin play. The top two teams in each round-robin pool will advance to a playoff round to determine the overall winners in each division. Teams in round-robin pools will be ranked according to standard USAP round-robin criteria: number of overall wins, head-to-head wins, overall point differential, then head-to-head point differential.

Prizes:

  • Advanced bracket players compete for a cash prize purse.
  • Other bracket players win DCPL T-shirts for 1st and 2nd places.

No free pizza this year, but we are partnering with Atlas Brew Works, which will be offering Happy Hour all day long.

Tournament location:

  Anacostia Recreation Center Outdoor Tennis Courts
  1800 Anacostia Dr, Washington, DC 20020

Used Paddle Donation:

We’re collecting used pickleball paddles for local high school students. Any paddle donations are greatly appreciated!

Lost items:

Several items (paddles and water bottles, etc.) were left behind at the tournament. If you lost something, contact us so that we can return these items to you!
